Showcase your Talent

Beyond Business is an annually-held case competition for high school students, by high school students. Beyond Business serves as a platform for aspiring entrepreneurs to improve their problem solving skills by solving real world challenges that companies face. The skills that participants develop from the competition apply beyond business.

Build your Confidence

When you go on the stage to present your solution, it's not just the solution that we look into - it's the way you present your solution. By speaking in public, you become confident.

Meet Other Entrepreneurs

Case Competitions arent only about creating solutions and winning prizers - they are about meeting other like-minded entrepreneurs as well! Beyond Business offers an exclusive opportunity to network with other leaders.
